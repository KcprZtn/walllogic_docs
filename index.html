Privacy Policy for WallLogic App

Last updated: 15.03.2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains the rules for processing and protecting the personal data of WallLogic app users.

2. Collected Data and Purpose of Processing

  • Location: The WallLogic app collects user location data, including background location data when the app is closed or not in use. This data is used exclusively to determine current weather conditions, which allows for automatic device wallpaper adjustments.
  • Advertising Data: The app uses the Google AdMob advertising network, which may collect device identifiers and traffic data to display personalized ads.

3. Data Sharing with Third Parties

Collected information may be shared with external service providers, specifically Google LLC as part of the Google AdMob service, in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. User Rights

The user has full control over their data. Consent for location tracking can be withdrawn or modified at any time in the device operating system settings. Consent for ad personalization can also be modified in Google account settings.
