Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01.04.2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains the rules for processing and protecting the personal data of WallLogic app users.

2. Collected Data and Purpose of Processing

  • Location: The WallLogic app collects user location data, including background location data when the app is closed or not in use. This data is used exclusively to determine current weather conditions, which allows for automatic device wallpaper adjustments.
  • Advertising Data: The app uses the Google AdMob advertising network, which may collect device identifiers, crash logs, diagnostic information, and traffic data to display personalized ads and analyze app performance.
  • Purchase History: If you make an in-app purchase (e.g., removing ads), your purchase history is collected and processed through the Google Play Billing system to verify your premium access.

3. Data Sharing with Third Parties

Collected information may be shared with external service providers to ensure the app's functionality and monetization:

  • Google LLC (AdMob & Play Billing): For the purpose of ad revenue generation, analytics, and managing in-app purchases in accordance with their privacy policies.
  • Norwegian Meteorological Institute (api.met.no): Anonymous location coordinates are shared strictly and exclusively to fetch accurate global weather forecast components necessary for the app's core feature.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

The app does not store your location data or personal identifiers on any developer-owned servers; everything is processed ephemerally on external APIs or stored locally on your device.

Data held by third parties (like Google AdMob or Google Play) goes by their retention schedules. Users can request the deletion of their advertising data or modify ad personalization directly within their Google Account settings.

5. User Rights

The user has full control over their data. Consent for location tracking can be withdrawn or modified at any time in the device operating system settings. The app will continue to function without location access, but weather-based rules will be temporarily disabled.
